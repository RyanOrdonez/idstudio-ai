Frequently Asked Questions

IDStudio.ai is an AI-powered interior design platform that helps designers create stunning mood boards, generate design ideas, and streamline their workflow. Our AI assistant analyzes your preferences and project requirements to provide personalized design suggestions, color palettes, and style recommendations.

We offer a 14-day free trial for all paid plans. No credit card is required to start your trial. You'll have access to all premium features during the trial period. After 14 days, you can choose to upgrade to a paid plan or continue with our free tier.

The Free plan includes basic mood board creation and limited AI suggestions. The Starter plan ($29/month) adds unlimited projects, advanced AI features, and client collaboration tools. The Pro plan ($79/month) includes everything plus priority support, advanced analytics, and white-label options.

Yes! You can upload your own photos, material samples, and inspiration images. Our AI will analyze these uploads to better understand your style preferences and provide more personalized design suggestions that match your aesthetic.

Our collaboration features allow you to share mood boards and projects with clients via secure links. Clients can view designs, leave comments, and provide feedback without needing an account. You can also generate professional presentation documents to share offline.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period, and you can export all your data before the subscription ends.
