About IDStudio.ai

We're revolutionizing interior design with cutting-edge AI technology, empowering designers to create stunning spaces faster than ever before.

Our Mission

At IDStudio.ai, we believe that exceptional interior design should be accessible to everyone. Our AI-powered platform democratizes the design process, giving both professional designers and homeowners the tools they need to create beautiful, functional spaces.

We're not here to replace human creativity – we're here to amplify it. Our AI assistant works alongside designers, providing instant inspiration, generating mood boards, and streamlining the entire design workflow.

Our Story

Founded by a team of designers and technologists, IDStudio.ai was born from the frustration of spending countless hours on repetitive design tasks. We saw an opportunity to harness the power of artificial intelligence to free up designers' time for what they do best – creating beautiful, meaningful spaces.

Since our launch, we've helped thousands of designers streamline their workflow, improve client communication, and deliver exceptional results faster than ever before.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at IDStudio.ai

Human-Centered

Technology should enhance human creativity, not replace it. We build tools that empower designers to do their best work.

Privacy First

Your designs and client data are yours. We use enterprise-grade security to protect your work and maintain your trust.

Continuous Innovation

We're constantly improving our AI models and adding new features based on designer feedback and industry trends.
